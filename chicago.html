River in Reverse: A Cultural History of the Chicago River


Completion: Ongoing


River in Reverse is a Substack that traces the deep connection between Chicago's urban waterways and the city's economic and cultural development. From understanding the city's history, the blog explores innovative ideas on building equitable, climate-resilient, and ecologically vibrant cities that foster community and feel like home.
